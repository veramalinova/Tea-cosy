The lost souls of Trekroner

July 2012

Director: Morten K. Roesen/Anne Sophie Gertz

Set, costume and light design: Nikolaus Webern / Photos: Mathias Løvgreen Bojesen

Singers: Regina Unnur Olafsdottir, Tobias Ørum, Nana Bugge Rasmussen, Martin Hatlo / Musicians: Live Berger Brekke, Nikolaj Koch Roesen / Performers: Maja Clementsen Hansen, Katrine Elise Leth Nielsen

Site specific opera production for the Copenhagen Opera Festival. It takes place on the old military fortress Trekroner in the Copenhagen habour. The audience is invited on a journey of discovery into the dark mind where restless souls, insane delusions and hellish torments lurk. A devilish pact surrounds the characters on the island of Trekroner – but can the audience penetrate the darkness to solve the mysteries of the island or are the souls forever lost?
